Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy?
Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of receipt if they are in original condition, unused, and with all original packaging and tags intact. Some items, like perishable goods and personalized products, may not be eligible for return.
When will I receive my order?
You can expect to receive your order within 5-7 business days for standard shipping. For expedited shipping, orders typically arrive within 2-3 business days. Please note that processing times may vary, and some items may take longer to ship.
